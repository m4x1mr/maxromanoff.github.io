Research

Two subscription paradigms emerged from competitive analysis

"Subscribe now" (Amazon-style) vs. "Add to cart to subscribe" (Safeway-style). Both were viable options for PC Express.

06
07

Design

Testing showed no user preference—so we optimized for constraints

I mapped both models against tech and business constraints. We chose the model with fewer dependencies and better checkout integration.

10

Challenge

Late discovery: $35 minimum order + no overlapping orders

These constraints almost defeated the program's purpose. I proposed merging subscriptions with open orders, or consolidating to the same day to maximize order value.

11
12

Outcome

Launched with strong marketing push across Canada

Despite constraints, we delivered a subscription program to millions of customers. The constraints impacted long-term success, validating our earlier concerns.
