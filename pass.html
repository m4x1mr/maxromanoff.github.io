Combatting our high delivery fees

We were hearing from our customers, time and time again, that fees were a major factor for churn. It was no secret that we wanted to establish a subscription for PC Express to both offer a way out for our customers, as well as outline a new revenue stream for our business.

We usability tested the experience at the end of the design sprint and realized we needed to be more aggressive with the branding as we saw banner blindness was going to be an issue. What I learned

In leading the PC Express Pass project, I addressed challenges from inception to launch.
